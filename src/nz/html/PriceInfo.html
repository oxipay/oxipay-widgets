Who can use Oxipay?

Anyone who is a New Zealand resident, at least 18 years old, has a valid and verifiable email address, a New Zealand mobile number, a New Zealand issued Visa or Mastercard credit or debit card, has a New Zealand residential and shipping address and has a New Zealand Drivers Licence.

For more information please visit www.oxipay.co.nz
